Philisophy

Founder's Tribe is an exclusive group of companies working together to help each other succeed. We are all experienced founders and executives who have been on the front line building teams, products, and revenues. We understand how to execute at all stages of a business, and collectively, we have raised over $500M in venture and debt financing. If you are accepted into the Tribe, we will help you with market strategy, product design, development, marketing, hiring, legal and fundraising. Each team contributes equity into a pool that is shared with the other members. When you join us, you have an interest in the success of every member of the Tribe and we in yours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do we join the Tribe?

You send us an email with your investor deck; we will review your deck and get in touch with you within a few days about the process.

How much of my equity goes into the Tribe?

You provide 10% of your equity into the Tribe.

How much in funding do we get when we join the Tribe?

It depends on the nature and stage of your deal. We fund companies with as small as $25K to as large a check as $1M through our network of investors.

What other services do we get by joining the Tribe?

You get access to our members who have experience on all aspects of startups from product strategy, go to market strategy, marketing, website development, legal formation, fundraising, business model design, pricing and so on.

About Us

Mehdi Maghsoodnia

I am a designer, engineer and system thinker at my core. I see the world in terms of design and when something does not work the way you would expect it, it is to me an opportunity to make it better. I have learned through the years that not every problem is worth solving and your ability to solve a problem is a complex function of the team, the tools and platforms available and market timing.

I love building teams and seeing those teams achieve amazing results. I have seen it many times; when you empower people and give them a vision of what the future should look like, the results are insane. There is no limit to how creative and resourceful a team of people can be if they believe in the mission and the story of how the world should be after they are done fixing it.

I created combustion to help founders find their momentum in the market faster by partnering with real operating executives and other founders. I also wanted to create a shared collective among different startups so that each founding team has a higher chance of success by investing in other startups with their equity.

You can read about my past on linkedIn if you are interested. I live in Menlo Park and enjoy playing soccer, biking, reading and spending time with family and friends.
